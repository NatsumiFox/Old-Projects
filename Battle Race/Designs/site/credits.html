Natsumi
Music, credits screen, level select, respawn system, minigame engine, gameplay, bugfixes
Natsumi has been responsible for most of the gameplay decisions, she has fixed a heap load of bugs present in the original Sonic 3 & Knuckles title, some bugs you wouldn't even know about, and has made the game 2-player compatible and as fair as reasonably practical. She helped build upon and expand the Box System, making it more flexible and dynamic than ever before, and created the respawn system, whilst setting up the directions for all levels in the game, and having created all mini-games available in the demo. She has contributed her fair share of music and remixes to the project, as well as being decisive in the game's direction and playback.
MarkeyJester
Graphics, music, screens, main menu, sound test, level select, Box System
MarkeyJester has spent considerable amount of time to create a nice and well polished presentation to the hack. He has created many art assets, the pause menu, most of the custom screens and all. He also created the heart of the hack; The Box System. This has made it possible to decide which player is ahead, and where the arrow will point at any time. The level select was mostly worked by him, and for a very long time; It was very complicated and difficult system to create, but because of it, the hack looks absolutely amazing and unique! He has also helped with many important ideas for the hack, which have made sure that the hack is just that much more fun to play.
